## Available Sectors (Use These Exact Names)

### 1. Advisory Services
**When to select**: Companies providing cryptocurrency investment advice, regulatory consulting, compliance services, or strategic advisory for blockchain projects.

### 2. Broker-Dealer Services  
**When to select**: Companies acting as intermediaries for buying/selling virtual assets, providing brokerage services, or facilitating trades between parties.

### 3. Custody Services
**When to select**: Companies providing wallet services, private key management, institutional custody, or secure storage of virtual assets.

### 4. Exchange Services
**When to select**: Companies operating trading platforms, spot exchanges, derivatives exchanges, or peer-to-peer trading services.

### 5. Lending and Borrowing Services
**When to select**: Companies providing cryptocurrency loans, yield farming, staking services, or collateralized lending.

### 6. Virtual Asset Management and Investment Services
**When to select**: Companies managing crypto funds, providing portfolio management, or operating investment vehicles for virtual assets.

### 7. Virtual Asset Derivative Services
**When to select**: Companies offering futures contracts, options trading, perpetual swaps, or other derivative instruments.

### 8. Virtual Asset Transfer and Settlement
**When to select**: Companies providing payment processing, cross-border transfers, remittance services, or settlement solutions.

### 9. Fiat Referenced Token Issuance Services
**When to select**: Companies issuing stablecoins, fiat-pegged tokens, or managing reserves for digital currencies.

### 10. Asset Referenced Token Issuance Services
**When to select**: Companies tokenizing real estate, commodities, securities, or other physical/financial assets.
